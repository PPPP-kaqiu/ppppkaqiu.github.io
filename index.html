I am a final-year master's student at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Tong Xu and Prof. Enhong Chen.

I'm currently a research intern at Baidu (ERNIE Star), supervised by Jing Liu and Haifeng Wang, focusing on cutting-edge research in LLM reasoning. I regularly share technical insights on Rednote—follow me for updates!

Previously, I interned at StepFun as a core contributor to Step2-mini, Step3 (under Houyi Li and Xiangyu Zhang), where I gained comprehensive LLM expertise across the full development cycle: data, pretraining (major), long-context, post-training and reasoning systems. This role also gave me hands-on experience with large-scale distributed training across one thousand GPUs.

My research interests span the full LLM stack (pre-training, reinforcement learning, and framework design). I believe these areas are connected by a unifying inference paradigm. I am looking for full-time opportunities in LLM foundational model teams at top tech companies, with a focus on LLM pre-training and reasoning systems.
